Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup salted butter cut into cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tsp vanilla
  • 3 tsp oil

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350f
  2. grease 2 8" cake pans and line with parchment
  3. whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl
  4. beat eggs for 30 sec
  5. pour sugar in over 45 sec
  6. beat for 7 min until tripled in volume and white
  7. place butter and milk in a heatproof container and microwave for 2 minutes do not let milk foam
  8. add flour to eggs in thirds and mix until the last bit is just mixed in
  9. add milk, vanilla, and oil into a bowl. add some egg batter and whisk until smooth
  10. slowly pour milk into the egg mixture
  11. scrape and mix until batter is smooth and pourable
  12. pour into pans and bake for 30 min or until golden
