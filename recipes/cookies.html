Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 375
  2. mix flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder in a bowl
  3. cream butter and sugar in another bowl
  4. beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy
  5. mix dry ingredients until combined
  6. add chocolate chips
  7. rolls dough into balls and place evenly on baking sheet
  8. bake for 8-10 minutes
  9. let sit before moving to cooling rack
