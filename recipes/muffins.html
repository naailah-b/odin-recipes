Muffins

I love blueberry muffins.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups blueberries
  • 3 tsp sugar

Steps

  1. preheat over to 375
  2. cream butter and sugar until light
  3. add eggs one at a time, then vanilla
  4. sift flour, salt, baking powder, and add to creamed mixture alternating with milk
  5. crush 1/2 blueberries with a fork and mix into batter, fold in the rest
  6. fill a muffin tin with batter
  7. bake for 30-35 min
